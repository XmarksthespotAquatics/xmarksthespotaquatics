❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How are the fish shipped internationally? +
Fish are carefully packed in oxygen-filled bags with water conditioner and insulated boxes to maintain temperature. We ship via trusted air cargo services experienced in live fish transport. Shipping typically takes 1–3 days internationally depending on your location.
What is your Dead on Arrival (DOA) policy? +
Local orders: Full live arrival guarantee. Any DOA must be reported with clear photo evidence within 2 hours of delivery.

International orders: A clear uncut video of the unopened bag must be sent within 2 hours of customs release or delivery. Claims without video evidence or submitted after this window cannot be processed. All fish are photographed in healthy condition before packing. Verified DOA claims will be handled via replacement or store credit.
What water parameters are your fish raised in? +
Our balloon mollies are raised in hard, slightly alkaline water: Temperature 26–28°C, pH 7.5–8.2, Hardness 10–20 dGH, with light salt (0.5–1 tsp per gallon). When acclimating to your tank, do so gradually over 30–45 minutes. Sudden changes in pH or hardness can cause stress — match parameters as closely as possible for best results.
Can I see the actual fish before I pay? +
Yes! Once we select your fish we send you photos and a short video of the actual fish before you confirm payment. You are buying a strain and trusting our selection — but we always show you what you are getting before it ships.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept GCash, PayPal, bank transfer, and Western Union. For international orders PayPal or bank transfer is recommended. Payment is confirmed after you approve the fish photos and before we begin packing.
What is the difference between the grades? +
Material Grade fish are healthy quality fish great for breeding programs. Show Potential fish are young fish (2.5–3cm) showing strong competition characteristics. Show Grade fish fully meet the balloon molly competition standard in body shape, scales, and coloration and are ready to enter shows at 3cm and above.
Do you ship to my country? +
We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping requirements and import regulations vary by country — some require permits or health certificates for live fish. Message us with your country and we will let you know the requirements and estimated shipping cost.
How do I care for balloon mollies after they arrive? +
Float the bag in your tank for 15–20 minutes to equalize temperature, then gradually add tank water to the bag before releasing. Maintain water temperature between 24–28°C and pH 7.0–8.0 in a well-filtered tank. We include a basic care guide with every shipment.
