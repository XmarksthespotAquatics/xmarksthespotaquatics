Frequently Asked Questions

How are fish shipped internationally? +
Fish are packed in pure oxygen-filled bags with water conditioner inside insulated cargo boxes. We ship via trusted air cargo services experienced in live fish transport. Typical transit: 1–3 days internationally.
What is your DOA (Dead on Arrival) policy? +
Local: Full live arrival guarantee — report with clear photo within 2 hours of delivery.
International: An uncut video of the sealed bag must be sent within 2 hours of customs release. Claims without video cannot be processed. Verified DOA claims handled via replacement or store credit.
Can I see the fish before I pay? +
Yes — once selected, we send you photos and a short video of your specific fish before you confirm payment. You see exactly what you're getting.
What payment methods do you accept? +
GCash, PayPal, bank transfer, and Western Union. For international orders PayPal or bank transfer is recommended. Payment is processed after you approve the fish photos.
What water parameters are your fish raised in? +
Temperature 26–28°C, pH 7.5–8.2, hardness 10–20 dGH, light salt (0.5–1 tsp/gallon). When acclimating, take 30–45 minutes to avoid stress from parameter changes.
Do you ship to my country? +
We ship to most countries worldwide. Some require live fish import permits or health certificates — message us with your country for specific requirements and shipping estimates.
How do I acclimate balloon mollies on arrival? +
Float the sealed bag in your tank for 15–20 minutes to equalize temperature. Gradually add tank water before releasing. Maintain 24–28°C, pH 7.0–8.0 in a well-filtered, established tank. A care guide is included with every shipment.
